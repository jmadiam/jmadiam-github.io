Why I chose MIS

12 Jan 2023

Raise your hand if your degree you chose was not your first choice.

Like most everyone else reading this, MIS was not my first major. Starting with Biology, I went to school and got okay grades for a single year. Feeling guilty about the money being spent on me, I took time off to reevaluate. I went back to school a year later with reinvented passion. But once again, I found that what I was doing I did not enjoy.

I am still trying to find that passion. My dream of working in a hospital lost its shine after working in a radiology department as a student tech. I was good at it, but the crowd was what threw me off. Everyone was so introverted. So, suprise, I took a major whose demographic you would expect to be even more introverted hahaha. But her I am, jumping head first into the world MIS.
